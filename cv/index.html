My Professional Core

I am a Front-end Engineer with 15+ years of experience. I speak design and code — and I understand the business behind both.

These three qualities define how I work and become more decisive in the AI era:

  • Product-Oriented: I take personal ownership of what I build, from first idea to production. And I put too much effort into my work not to care how it turns out.
  • Big-Picture Thinker: I make decisions through full context: business, technical, human. Not gut feeling — even smart people fall into cognitive traps.
  • Perfectionist: I care deeply about the quality of my craft. If it ships with my name on it, the bar has to be high.

They matter because they're exactly what AI agents lack. Agents can generate code, but they can't own outcomes. They can process data, but they can't grasp the full context of a business. They can follow instructions, but they can't set the bar for quality. That's what I bring to the table.

Are We the Right Fit?

Here is a quick, honest look at how I work, to help us figure out if we're a good fit for each other.

We are a GREAT FIT if...
We are NOT A FIT if...

Dedication to Success

We are a GREAT FIT if...

You prioritize product success and personally advocate for the quality and impact of the final outcome.

We are NOT A FIT if...

You are pretty indifferent to product success and lack conviction in the long-term vision or value of the results.

Technical Breadth

We are a GREAT FIT if...

You value "T-shaped" engineers who leverage strong fundamentals to master any tech stack and see the big picture.

We are NOT A FIT if...

You seek a narrow specialist with focused expertise in one specific framework for a limited scope of work.

Product Complexity

We are a GREAT FIT if...

You build complex interfaces where a polished, intuitive user experience creates competitive advantage.

We are NOT A FIT if...

You need basic CRUD forms where visual polish and user satisfaction do not drive business value.

Sustainable Balance

We are a GREAT FIT if...

You treat software as a marathon. You balance rapid delivery with a robust, sustainable foundation.

We are NOT A FIT if...

You manage by emergency. A "ship it yesterday" mindset consistently prioritizes speed over technical health.

AI & Innovation

We are a GREAT FIT if...

You adopt AI thoughtfully: maximizing its potential while managing practical limits and security risks.

We are NOT A FIT if...

You swing between extremes: either avoiding AI entirely or trusting it blindly without technical oversight.

Purpose & Impact

We are a GREAT FIT if...

You see profit as the baseline but are driven by a product's global purpose and positive impact on users.

We are NOT A FIT if...

You prioritize profit over value and remain indifferent to the actual human impact your product delivers.

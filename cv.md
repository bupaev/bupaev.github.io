## Are We the Right Fit?

Here is a quick, honest look at how I work, to help us figure out if we're a good fit for each other.

### Dedication to Success

**We are a GREAT FIT if...** You prioritize product success and personally advocate for the quality and impact of the final outcome.

**We are NOT A FIT if...** You are pretty indifferent to product success and lack conviction in the long-term vision or value of what you're building.

### Technical Breadth

**We are a GREAT FIT if...** You value "T-shaped" engineers who leverage strong fundamentals to master any tech stack and see the bigger architectural picture.

**We are NOT A FIT if...** You seek a narrow specialist with focused expertise in one specific framework for a limited scope of work.

### Product Complexity

**We are a GREAT FIT if...** You build complex interfaces where a polished, intuitive user experience creates competitive advantage and drives business value.

**We are NOT A FIT if...** You need basic CRUD forms where visual polish and user satisfaction do not drive business value.

### Sustainable Balance

**We are a GREAT FIT if...** You treat software as a marathon. You balance rapid delivery with a robust, sustainable foundation.

**We are NOT A FIT if...** You manage by emergency. A "ship it yesterday" mindset consistently prioritizes speed over technical excellence and team wellbeing.

### AI & Innovation

**We are a GREAT FIT if...** You adopt AI thoughtfully: maximizing its potential while managing practical limits and security risks.

**We are NOT A FIT if...** You swing between extremes: either avoiding AI entirely or trusting it blindly without technical oversight.

### Purpose & Impact

**We are a GREAT FIT if...** You see profit as the baseline but are driven by a product's global purpose and positive impact on users.

**We are NOT A FIT if...** You prioritize profit over value and remain indifferent to the actual human impact your product delivers.
